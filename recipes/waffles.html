Classic Waffles

Description

A toasty, crispy waffle. The perfect treat to start your morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tsps baking powder
  • 2 tbsps white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture, beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
