Oatmeal Cookies

Description

Grandma's cookies have never been better. One bite of these will leave your mouth wanting more, with every bit showering your tastebuds in warm love.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup softened butter
  • 3/4 cup white sugaar
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 3/4 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, brown sugar until smooth. Beat the eggs and vanilla until fluffy. Stir together flour, bakin gsoda, cinnamon, and salt. Gradually beat into butter mixture. Stir in oats and raisins. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  3. Bake 8-10 minutes in the oven, or until golden brown. Cool slightly, remove from sheet to wire rack. Cool completely.
